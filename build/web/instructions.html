SQLDraw is a database modeling application, based on the Entity - Relationship Model. How to use:

  • Creating an entity/attribute: Click on any of the buttons Entity, Weak Entity, Attribute, Derived Attribute, Multivalued Attribute. You will be asked to provide a name.

  • Rename an entity/attribute by clicking on the Rename button, and then click on the object you want to rename.

  • Connect an attribute to its owner with the Connect Attribute button. First click on the attribute and then on its owner. (In this order.)

  • Deleting an object: Click on the Delete button, then click on the object you want to delete.

  • You can move an object around with click & drag. To resize a shape, double-click on it once and drag to any direction. Double-click again to stop resizing.

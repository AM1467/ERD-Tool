SQL Draw is a database modeling application, based on the Entity - Relationship Model.

How to use:

  • Creating an entity/attribute/relationship: Click on any of the buttons under the New submenu. You will be asked to provide a name.

  • Rename/Delete an object by clicking on the Rename button, and then click on the object you want to rename or delete.

  • Connect an attribute to its owner with the Set Attribute Owner button. First click on the attribute and then on its owner. (In this order.)

  • Change an attribute's properties by clicking on a property (for example "Unique") and click on the corresponding attribute.

  • Connect a relationship with an entity by using the buttons under the Relationships submenu. First click on the relationship shape and then on the entity. (In this order.)

  • You can move an object around with click & drag. To resize a shape, double-click on it once and drag to any direction. Double-click again to stop resizing.
